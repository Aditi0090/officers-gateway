About

Founded in 2024, OfficersGateway is an educational platform and publication dedicated to providing quality content to students preparing for government examinations through YouTube channels and publications.

We are proud to introduce map-based learning books for better understanding and retention. Our books simplify concepts through visuals, diagrams and attractive layouts.

OfficersGateway delivers specially designed books for SSC, Bihar Exams, Railway, BPSC and other competitive examinations using visual learning techniques.

About the Founder

Aditya Kumar Manish, founder of OfficersGateway, has cleared multiple competitive examinations and designed innovative learning books focused on visual and smart preparation.
